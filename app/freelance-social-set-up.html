Freelance Socials Set-up

Set up client on appropriate social media platforms to help increase awareness of their products and boost sales.

Live Link

Process

I set this client up with an Instagram and Tiktok account after some market research around similar business showed these would be the most popular within their target market. I created templates for Instagram posts in line with their branding to help create a recognisable theme and produced regular TikTok video themes for contant user generatable content.

Outcome

My client was very happy with their social media accounts which gained over 200 organic followers within the first month and 5 online orders.
