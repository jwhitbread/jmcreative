BOHS Email Marketing Updates

Invigorate the companies monthly e-bulletin and incorporate the updated branding and logo.

Process

Using Mailchimp and Canva I created a bright and engaging e-bulletin that included branded headers to help each content section stand out whilst also keeping to the brief. The use of Mailchimp meant I could save this style as a template for the company to use for future communications to ensure they stay on brand.

Outcome

The client was really happy with the updated e-bulletin and has received comments from customers about the refreshed, engaging style.
